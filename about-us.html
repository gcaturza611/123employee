123Employee was founded in 2006 to meet the needs of small and enterprise level businesses.

123Employee is indeed the premiere outsourcing authority you can trust! Since then we have grown to a team of hundreds of outsourced agents. We set out to create a company that would make outsourcing affordable to all businesses while offering high-level training for our employees. We help businesses all over the globe increase productivity and lower expenses. We provide business process outsourcing, specializing in online and offline marketing, customer service, and virtual assistant services from our three outsourcing centers. Our company features fully functional voice and data, enterprise-grade network infrastructure, backup power generators, and other reliable features you can count on.

"Our Core Values"

At 123Employee, we believe in fostering a culture that reflects our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction. Our core values shape our actions, define our identity, and drive our success. Here’s what we stand for:

1. Client-Centric Approach: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, understand, and tailor our services to meet their unique needs. Your success is our success.

2. Continuous Learning and Training: Our team of over 350 virtual assistants receives ongoing training to stay ahead of industry trends. We believe in investing in our employees’ development to provide top-notch service to our clients.

3. Philippines-Based Excellence: Our center is strategically located in the Philippines. Why? Because of the language skills, light accent, and affinity for American culture. We create opportunities for people while helping businesses increase productivity and lower expenses.
